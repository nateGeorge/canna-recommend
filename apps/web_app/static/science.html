Science is cool

The endocannabinoid system of the human body has CB1 and CB2 receptors to which THC and CBD bind. This causes effects in the body, such as being "high" with THC and alleviating pain and anxiety with CBD.

My research into the cannabis field so far has found that both chemistry and people's reviews of strains tend to group into 3 groups, based on silhoutte scores of k-means clustering. Two of the clusters are somewhat similar, and a third, much smaller cluster is high-CBD strains like AC/DC and Harlequin. You can see some of the clustering below, and a histogram of the total THC of the three groups clustered by chemistry. Interestingly, THC and CBD have the same chemical composition, and only differ by one chemical bond.
